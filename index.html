4 Features
Low cost
Saving your bridge and swap fee.With PicWe users can use USDT on one blockchain trade any token on other chain.And It is totally bridgeless. Trading fees are lower than bridge.
Decentralized
Omni-Chain Trade directly from your wallet app with the smart contract.Different from centralized exchanges like Binance PicWe never hold your funds when you trade: you have 100% ownership of your own crypto.
Easy
Users don't need to learn complex multi-chain applications or master intricate techniques; they can complete transactions for assets on other chains with just one click.
Fast
The entire cross-chain transaction process takes less than a minute. As PicWe is upgraded and more users join the PicWe ecosystem, the cross-chain transaction time will continue to decrease. Ultimately, the cross-chain transaction time will approach the on-chain block time.
